My Hometown

Date Posted: 10/16/25

This blog post is about my hometown, San Francisco, California! I'm from the Sunset District, which is just south of Golden Gate Park. It's also where my high school, Abraham Lincoln HS, is. Here's a fun fact: it was the school that was used for a popular cartoon called The Amazing World of Gumball.

SF is a beautiful city that I think everybody should visit at least once in their life. We have good food, many tourist destinations, and even a great public transportation system called the Muni! I, like many others, didn't even start driving until near the end of high school because I always took the bus everywhere.

When you visit, make sure to bring some warm clothes because it's almost always foggy and windy here. I, personally, enjoy SLO's perfect weather more than I ever did SF's. To add to that, there's a local saying that "there are no seasons in San Francisco" because the weather is the same year-round. Through the summer, it remains cold. In the winter, it doesn't snow. (Actually, there have only been a handful of times when snow was recorded, the last time being 1976).

Unfortunately, San Francisco lies in the San Andreas Fault and has had severe earthquakes in the past. The next one is predicted to have a magnitude of 6.7 or larger within the next 30 years.

Despite the danger, it's still a city that I'll always be proud to be from. If you don't love it already, I hope that someday you will get to visit and see why people say it's so great!
